Construction Sub-Contracting Management

Manage sub-contracting workflows for construction projects including labour requisitions, quotation comparison, work orders, task completion, and RA billing.

Key Features

  • Labour requisition and quotation comparison
  • Work order creation and execution
  • Task completion tracking
  • RA (Running Account) billing
  • Advance, debit, and credit recovery management
  • Retention management
  • Contractor payment reports
